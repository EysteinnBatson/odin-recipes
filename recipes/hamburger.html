Hamburger

The hamburger, man's second best friend.

In February, 1886 in rural Germany, Joseph Hamburger was trying to feed his family with leftover meat, chopped vegetables and bread.

It was when a business man they knew who was visiting was over for dinner one night when they had told him about this meal that the idea of this meal being carefully curated into a dish that started the hamburger's journey.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Chop the vegetables
  3. Heat the bread
  4. Add the meat
  5. Add the vegetables
  6. Serve with a side and a drink of your choice.
